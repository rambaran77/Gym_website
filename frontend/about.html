Our story

Built on grit, discipline & community

Aura Athletic was founded on a simple belief: everyone deserves access to a great gym — no matter where you come from. We're more than equipment and classes; we're a place where ordinary people become extraordinary.

Founder & owner

Ram Baran Yadav

Founder · Owner · Aura Athletic

I grew up in a middle-class family where nothing was handed to us — every opportunity was earned through study, discipline, and showing up when it mattered. That mindset shaped who I am today and why I built Aura Athletic.

After graduating with a degree in Computer Science from University College Birmingham, I built on the skills and structure that higher education provides. Technology opened doors, but fitness kept me grounded — the gym became my second home, a place to reset, push limits, and build confidence away from the screen.

Today I'm building my next chapter as an aspiring Cybersecurity Analyst, protecting systems and data in a world that runs on technology. Running Aura Athletic alongside that journey keeps me balanced: the same focus, resilience, and problem-solving I bring to security, I bring to every member who walks through our doors.
