Common questions

Do I need a contract?

No long-term contract on standard monthly memberships. Day and weekly passes are one-off purchases.

Can I cancel my membership?

Yes — manage your plan from My membership in your account. Access continues until your paid period ends.

Are classes included?

Selected weekly and monthly plans include unlimited group classes. Day passes can add a class for a small upgrade.
